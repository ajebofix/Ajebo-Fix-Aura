Vehicle Health Status — Explained

Your vehicle’s Health Status provides a high-level view of its current condition based on available records, observations, and monitored data.

It is designed to support informed decisions — not to replace professional inspection or diagnosis.

Stable

No significant risk indicators are currently present. Regular monitoring and routine care are advised.

Attention Required

Certain observations suggest increased risk. While not urgent, professional review is recommended to prevent escalation.

Critical Review Advised

Multiple or severe risk indicators are present. Immediate professional assessment is strongly recommended.

What influences the Health Status?

  • Maintenance and service records
  • Reported concerns and observations
  • Mileage consistency and usage patterns
  • System-detected risk indicators

Important Notice

Aura does not diagnose vehicle faults or prescribe repairs.

All insights are advisory and intended to guide responsible vehicle care. Final decisions and corrective actions should always follow professional inspection by Ajebo Fix.
