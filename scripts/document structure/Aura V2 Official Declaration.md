# Aura V2 Official Declaration

The Aura V2 Declaration should be the one document that every future feature, route, model, screen, advisor workflow, investor pitch, and AI behavior is measured against.

⸻

## AURA V2 OFFICIAL DECLARATION

Ajebo Fix Automotive Health Platform

Version: V2
Status: Officially Declared
Date: June 2026

⸻

## PREAMBLE

Aura exists to create a new category of automotive care.

Traditional automotive systems focus on repairs.

Aura focuses on continuity.

Traditional automotive businesses react to failures.

Aura is designed to monitor progression, structure care, preserve continuity, and support informed advisor-guided decisions.

Aura is not built to replace mechanics.

Aura is built to organize automotive health.

This declaration establishes the permanent identity, philosophy, boundaries, authority structures, and architectural protections of Aura V2. From this point forward, all development must align with this declaration.  ￼  ￼

⸻

## ARTICLE I

OFFICIAL IDENTITY

Aura Is

Aura is:

* A private automotive health management platform
* A vehicle continuity platform
* An automotive care coordination system
* A vehicle health monitoring environment
* A structured automotive record system
* An advisor-guided automotive care platform

Aura exists to help vehicle owners, drivers, and advisors maintain visibility, continuity, and oversight throughout a vehicle’s operational life.  ￼

⸻

Aura Is Not

Aura is not:

* A repair app
* A mechanic marketplace
* A workshop CRM
* A fault-code generator
* A repair instruction platform
* A DIY maintenance platform
* A diagnostic AI system
* A chatbot product

Any future feature that pushes Aura toward these categories should be rejected unless formally reviewed and approved.  ￼  ￼

⸻

## ARTICLE II

THE ROLE OF RINA

Rina Is

Rina is:

* A clinical automotive care assistant
* A continuity assistant
* A monitoring assistant
* An escalation assistant
* A context-aware vehicle health guide

Rina exists to support awareness, continuity, observation, and escalation.  ￼

⸻

Rina Is Not

Rina is not:

* A mechanic
* A repair instructor
* A diagnostic engine
* A replacement for professional review
* An autonomous decision-maker
* A repair authorization system

Rina must never be presented as a source of mechanical certainty. Human advisors remain the final authority.  ￼  ￼

⸻

## ARTICLE III

CORE PRODUCT PRINCIPLES

Every Aura feature must support one or more of the following principles:

Continuity

Vehicle history must remain connected across time.

Monitoring

The system should observe progression, not merely record events.

Structure

Concerns, consultations, treatment plans, and records must remain organized.

Escalation

The platform should guide appropriate review when risk increases.

Authority

Advisor authority remains protected and visible.

Calm

The platform should reduce anxiety and increase confidence.

Trust

Every interaction should reinforce professional oversight and operational clarity.  ￼

⸻

## ARTICLE IV

OFFICIAL USER ROLES

Aura V2 recognizes four primary operational roles:

Owner

May:

* View vehicle health
* View records
* Access consultations
* Access treatment progress
* Interact with Rina

May not:

* Modify treatment states
* Access advisor tools
* Access administrative systems

⸻

Driver

May:

* Report concerns
* View operational status
* Request escalation
* Interact with Rina

May not:

* Control treatment plans
* Access financial controls
* Access advisor tools

⸻

Advisor

May:

* Review consultations
* Create assessments
* Create treatment plans
* Escalate concerns
* Manage vehicle continuity

⸻

Admin

Maintains full operational authority across the platform.  ￼

⸻

## ARTICLE V

PROTECTED SYSTEMS

The following systems are officially protected and form the foundation of Aura V2.

Clinical Records Layer

* Conversation Records
* Consultation Records
* Assessment Records
* Vehicle Health Records

⸻

Treatment Layer

* Treatment Plans
* Treatment States
* Treatment Progression
* Monitoring States

⸻

Care Layer

* Care Plans
* Priority Access
* Monitoring Access
* Escalation Access

⸻

Operational Layer

* Admin Console
* Advisor CRM
* Consultation Queue
* Fleet Health Monitoring

⸻

Vehicle Health Layer

* Concerns
* Events
* Alerts
* Health Snapshots
* Vehicle Timelines

Changes to these systems require documented architectural review.  ￼

⸻

## ARTICLE VI

CLINICAL CONVERSATION STANDARD

Conversations are not treated as disposable chat.

Aura treats conversations as operational records.

Meaningful interactions must support:

* Concern tracking
* Escalation awareness
* Advisor continuity
* Vehicle history
* Risk progression

Aura records meaning, not merely messages.  ￼

⸻

## ARTICLE VII

TREATMENT PLAN STANDARD

Treatment Plans exist to manage progression.

Treatment Plans do not exist to provide repair instructions.

Aura communicates:

* Status
* Progression
* Monitoring
* Continuity

Aura does not communicate workshop-level repair procedures to clients.  ￼

⸻

## ARTICLE VIII

CARE PLAN PHILOSOPHY

Aura does not monetize fear.

Aura monetizes:

* Continuity
* Monitoring
* Oversight
* Priority
* Confidence

Priority Access, Active Monitoring, and Preventive Coverage are operational care structures, not marketing gimmicks.  ￼  ￼

⸻

## ARTICLE IX

ADMIN AUTHORITY

The advisor remains the authority.

AI supports.

Humans decide.

Rina may:

* Observe
* Summarize
* Escalate
* Guide

Rina may not:

* Approve treatment
* Close treatment plans
* Override advisors
* Diagnose vehicles

Authority remains centralized within the advisor and administrative layers.  ￼

⸻

## ARTICLE X

DEVELOPMENT LAW

Effective immediately:

No feature may be added simply because it is interesting.

Every proposed feature must answer:

1. Does it support Aura’s clinical identity?
2. Does it strengthen continuity?
3. Does it fit the protected architecture?
4. Does it preserve advisor authority?
5. Does it reduce cognitive load?
6. Does it improve operational scale?

If the answer is no, the feature should not be built.  ￼  ￼

⸻

## ARTICLE XI

OFFICIAL PRODUCT STANDARD

Every Aura interaction should leave the user feeling:

“My vehicle is being professionally monitored.”

Never:

“I am chatting with a mechanic bot.”

Never:

“I am using a repair app.”

The emotional outcome is part of the product.  ￼

⸻

# FINAL DECLARATION

Aura V2 is hereby declared:

A private automotive healthcare operating system built around monitoring, continuity, escalation awareness, structured records, advisor-guided care, and professional operational oversight.

All future development shall preserve this identity.

Aura will be built system-by-system, not feature-by-feature.

Declared and Adopted by Ajebo Fix Automotive Health.
